Our Story

Born from the
frustration of every
midday shine.

It started in a Mumbai summer. Humidity at 85%. Kajal sliding. Foundation patchy. And a packet of foreign blotting papers that smelled like chemicals and left a waxy film.

Mattly was born from a simple question: why doesn't a product like this exist for us? Something that works with Indian skin—oilier in humidity, more melanin-rich, more diverse in texture—not against it.

We spent 18 months developing a proprietary rice-fibre matrix with dermatologists. No parabens. No synthetic fragrance. No compromise.

How It Works

Three seconds.
All-day freshness.

Blotting has never been simpler — or more effective.

Step 01
🤲

Pull one sheet

Each sheet is individually separated and ultra-thin — peel it away from the pack with one finger. No tearing, no mess.

Step 02
✨

Press, don't rub

Gently press the sheet against your T-zone or wherever you're shining. Hold for 2–3 seconds. Lift straight up — never drag.

Step 03
🌸

Reveal fresh skin

See the difference instantly. Oil is gone, makeup is intact, and your skin looks naturally matte — not powdered or flat.

What's Inside

Ingredients that earn
their place.

Every ingredient is chosen for a reason. Nothing synthetic. Nothing unnecessary.

🌾
Rice Paper

Our base. Traditionally used in East Asian skincare, unbleached rice fibres create the ideal matrix for rapid oil absorption without a waxy or powdery residue.

🍵
Green Tea Extract

Rich in polyphenols and EGCG, green tea naturally regulates sebum and calms skin inflammation — making it ideal for the Detox variant.

🖤
Activated Charcoal

A powerful adsorbent that binds to excess oil, dirt, and pollutants — drawing them away from pores. City-skin's best friend.

🏔️
Kaolin Clay

The gentlest of clays, kaolin absorbs oil while soothing and smoothing the skin surface — never drying, never stripping.
